Dashboard Guide

This section gives you a quick tour of the Lighthouse dashboard layout, menu, and how to navigate cards.

  • Menu bar: Top navigation for quick actions and user info.
  • Cards: Each card tracks an aspect of your creative work, energy, or check-in flow.
  • Footer: Contextual actions (like “Do a Rhythm Pulse”).
  • Everything is modular—rearrange cards or sections as your system evolves.
