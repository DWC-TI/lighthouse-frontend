Energy & Flow

The color system and creative flow chart:

  • Dots & lines:
    • Green (1–3): High energy & flow (top of chart)
    • Yellow (4–6): Medium energy (middle of chart)
    • Red (7–9): Low energy (bottom of chart)
  • Interpretation: The higher the dot, the better your flow; red signals you may want to rest or reset.
  • Method: The Lighthouse approach keeps you tuned into your natural rhythms—track, reflect, and adjust as needed.
