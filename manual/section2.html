Cards Explained

Every card in the dashboard serves a specific purpose. Here’s what they do:

  • Beacon Hero: Your focus anchor, current theme, or goal.
  • Builder Beam: Core actions or projects.
  • Maintenance: Admin, inbox, cleanup.
  • Exploration: Learning, ideation, new tools or skills.
  • Return Point: Your anchor for restarting, next cue or checkpoint.
  • Creative Flow Timeline: Visualizes your last 7 days’ momentum and energy with color coding.
  • Check-In: Quick log of your state, mood, and focus.
  • Weekly Beacon: Reflects your week and plans the next.
  • Blind Spot: Spot blockers, friction, or missed signals.
